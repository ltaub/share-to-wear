What is Share to Wear?

Bored of your closet? Nothing to wear? When we look good, we feel good. With Share to Wear, feel good all the time. We want you to have access to all of the items you'd ever want to wear - most of all, the cute clothes we see around the Midd campus every day!

Our Mission

Share to Wear stems from the idea: What if we could borrow clothes from every Middlebury student on campus? Share to Wear is a peer to platform clothing rental service with Middlebury students supplying the rental clothes. We're creating one large, accessible, fashionable, and affordable closet for all Middkids. We aim to foster community, inclusivity, and sustainability.
